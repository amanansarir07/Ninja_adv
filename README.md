## The World Underwater

The *entire world is underwater*: every level is submerged, with a blue-green water overlay and floating bubbles. **Aquaman** leads the fight to **save sea creatures from extinction**—they are disappearing from the sea, and Aquaman (alongside Naruto and Kakashi) battles through **Village Depths**, **Ruins Depths**,  **Temple Depths**, and Fire Fortress to clear the depths and protect ocean life.

## Short Description (1–2 sentences)

**Ninja Adventure** is a 2D side-scrolling action game built with Phaser 3 and Vite. The whole world is *underwater*—play as **Aquaman**, Naruto, or Kakashi and save sea creatures from extinction by fighting through four submerged stages.

## Full Game Description

Ninja Adventure

The ocean used to be full of life. Coral reefs were vibrant, fish moved in huge schools, and the underwater world felt peaceful. But something strange has started happening beneath the surface. Sea creatures are disappearing, and parts of the ocean are slowly dying.

Rumors say that a mysterious group has been building hidden laboratories deep underwater. They are running dangerous experiments that are poisoning the ocean and disturbing the balance of marine life.

In Ninja Adventure, you play as one of three powerful warriors — Aquaman, Naruto, or Kakashi — each with their own abilities and fighting style. Your mission is to dive deep into the ocean, fight through enemy forces, and stop the experiments before the damage becomes permanent.

The game is a 2D side-scrolling action platformer where you move through underwater environments, defeat enemies, and survive the dangers of the deep. Along the way you’ll have to manage your health and oxygen while using melee attacks and special abilities to clear each stage.

As you progress, the journey takes you deeper into the ocean. Each level becomes more dangerous, introducing stronger enemies, tougher bosses, and harsher environments. What starts in a flooded village eventually leads to ancient ruins, a mysterious underwater temple, and finally the enemy’s main fortress hidden far below the surface.

Each character brings something different to the fight. Some focus on speed and precision, while others rely on power or balanced combat. Choosing the right abilities and timing your attacks becomes important as enemies grow more aggressive.

The deeper you go, the more the truth behind these experiments begins to reveal itself. By the time you reach the final fortress, you’ll face the leaders responsible for everything happening beneath the ocean.

The goal is simple: fight through the depths, stop the enemy forces, and help restore balance to the underwater world.

Features

Three playable characters with unique abilities

Four underwater levels with increasing difficulty

Multiple enemy types and boss fights

Oxygen survival mechanic while exploring underwater environments

Melee combat with special skills and cooldowns

Hidden collectibles and environmental effects

Underwater visuals with bubbles, lighting, and parallax backgrounds

Controls

Movement: Arrow Keys / WASD
Punch: J
Kick: K
Skill 1: L
Skill 2: U
All stages are rendered with an underwater look: blue-green overlay and floating bubbles.  

Backgrounds use parallax (slower scroll than foreground). All levels are single-screen width in tiles but extend horizontally; the camera scrolls with the player.

### Win / Lose Conditions

- **Stage clear:** All Sound Ninja on the stage are defeated → Stage Clear screen → next stage (or title after Level 3).  
- **Game Over:** Player health ≤ 0 or player falls below the level → Game Over screen → retry same level.  
- **Game Complete:** Clear Level 3 → Game Complete screen → return to title.

## Technical Summary (Optional)

- **Engine:** Phaser 3 (JavaScript).  
- **Build:** Vite, ES modules.  
- **Resolution:** 1152×768 (scale mode: FIT, centered).  
- **Physics:** Arcade, 120 FPS cap, gravity on characters; no global world gravity.  
- **Scenes:** Preloader → Title → Character Select → Level 1 / 2 / 3 (with overlay UIScene for HUD, Pause, Victory, Game Over, Game Complete).
